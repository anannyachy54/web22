MAURITIUS

Mauritius is a small country with a big heart. Located off the south-eastern coast of Africa, this island nation is a magnet for enthusiastic lovers and seekers of a tropical haven. Mauritius is no stranger to luxury resorts. Indulge yourself with some of the finest things and amenities money can buy as you gaze into the vastness of the Indian Ocean. Outside the resorts, get your adrenaline running by choosing from a host of activities and sports. The bright and sublime waters encircling the island make for perfect conditions for some snorkelling or diving. If getting wet isn't your thing then relax with some quiet games of golf or horse-riding sessions. Make sure to try the local cuisine as well. Influenced by the French, Indians, Chinese and Creole communities, Mauritian food is a true representation of the many years of trade and cultural influx from other parts of the world.
